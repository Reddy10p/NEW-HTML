What is SIEM?

.SIEM stands for Security Information Event Management.

.It is a centralized management tool which collects real time logs from network devices, servers, applications, Databases etc.

.Once it collects the logs, it normalizes the logs and keeps the logs for long time for audit, compliance and forensic puposes .

As well as it gives searching and reporting feature. It also enables real time monitoring, correlation and alerting feature.
